Company Overview

Federal-contracting discipline. Dual clinical leadership.

Brain Fit, LLC is a multi-specialty provider network and behavioral health consulting firm headquartered in the Phoenix metropolitan area. Co-owned and clinically directed by Drs. Core Competencies

What Brain Fit delivers.

Eight core competency areas covering the full range of federal evaluation, clinical, and consulting work.

Provider Network Management
Nationwide sourcing, credentialing, scheduling, compliance tracking, and quality assurance across a multi-disciplinary licensed provider network.
SSA Disability Consultative Examinations
Psychological CEs for adult and child disability determinations through state Disability Determination Services (DDS) under federal SSA funding. 10+ years of full-time key personnel experience producing administrative-hearing-grade evaluation reports under 20 CFR 404.1519 / 416.919 evidentiary standards.
C&P Evaluation Delivery
Coordinated Compensation & Pension evaluations for veteran populations through VA and DoD-aligned programs; provider development and workforce readiness. Built on key personnel's foundation in methodologically parallel SSA consultative work.
Fitness-for-Duty & Regulatory Evaluation
Psychological assessment, structured reporting, and administrative-hearing-grade documentation for state regulatory boards (current Arizona Board of Nursing experience) and federal fitness-for-duty contexts, including security clearance and workforce readiness applications.
Clinical Assessment & Testing
Cognitive, personality, diagnostic, and risk assessment using validated instruments including the WAIS-5, WMS-V, WISC-V, WPPSI, WIAT-4, ADOS-2, CARS-2, Conners-4, Trail Making Test (Trails A and B), and Rey-15.
Behavioral Health Consulting
Design, implementation, and program evaluation of trauma-informed, recovery-oriented behavioral health programs for public-sector and healthcare partners.
Training & Workforce Development
Competency-based clinical training, supervision, and continuing education for behavioral health professionals across multi-site programs.
Project Management & Administration
Turn-key contract coordination, compliance tracking, quality assurance, scheduling, and data reporting across integrated systems.
