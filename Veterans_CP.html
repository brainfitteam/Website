Who we are

One team. Five specialties. A complete clinical ecosystem behind every evaluation.

Brain Fit, LLC is a multi-specialty provider network and federal evaluation contractor headquartered in Phoenix, Arizona. Co-owned by two licensed clinical psychologists with 10+ years of federal-grade disability evaluation experience, the firm credentials, coordinates, and manages a multi-disciplinary licensed provider network — handling the administrative, credentialing, and coordination work so our team members can focus entirely on the clinical evaluation.

The Brain Fit Team operates within an integrated clinical ecosystem that includes Trinity Tree, LLC (a multi-PsyD outpatient evaluation and training practice) and Desert Recovery Centers (a Joint Commission Gold Seal accredited residential and step-down treatment system). When you join Brain Fit, you become part of a clinical infrastructure that is substantially larger and more accountable than a typical evaluation network.

Why join the Brain Fit Team

What you gain as a team member

Steady referral pipeline
Brain Fit holds active federal and state evaluation contracts and pursues additional contracting pipelines across VA, SSA, and fitness-for-duty work. Team members gain access to evaluation work without sourcing their own contracts.
Full administrative support
Credentialing, scheduling, compliance, and reporting coordination are managed by the Brain Fit Team. You focus on the clinical evaluation; we handle the administrative burden around it.
Flexible engagement
Work evaluations around your existing practice. There is no minimum volume requirement to join the team. You set your availability and Brain Fit matches referrals to your schedule.
Meaningful clinical impact
Federal evaluations directly impact access to disability benefits, healthcare, and long-term support for veterans and disabled populations. Your work as a team member makes a real difference.
Competitive compensation
Team members are compensated competitively on a per-evaluation basis. Brain Fit is transparent about rates and payment timelines from the start of the relationship.
Supported onboarding
New team members receive orientation on federal evaluation protocols, DBQ and report formatting standards, and Brain Fit quality expectations so you feel confident before your first evaluation.
